What Does the Result Indicate?

The NDVI Change result displayed above represents the difference in vegetation health between the two years you selected.

Areas with positive NDVI change indicate an increase in vegetation health, whereas negative values suggest a decline.

This analysis helps in understanding the impact of environmental factors, land use changes, and agricultural practices over time.
